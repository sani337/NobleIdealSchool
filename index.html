Welcome to Noble Ideal High School

Welcome to Noble Ideal High School, one of the most esteemed and trusted centers of learning in Dhaka. Since its establishment in 1992, Noble Ideal School has stood as a beacon of excellence — dedicated to inspiring young minds, nurturing potential, and shaping the future of our nation through quality education and moral guidance.

At Noble Ideal High School, we believe that education is not merely about acquiring knowledge — it is about shaping character, nurturing creativity, and inspiring the spirit of lifelong learning. Our vision is to prepare each student to become a confident, responsible, and compassionate individual who contributes positively to society and the world at large.

Our dedicated faculty members are not only teachers but mentors who guide, inspire, and care deeply about each student’s success. They work tirelessly to create a supportive learning environment where students feel safe to explore their potential and dream big.

Beyond academics, Noble Ideal School emphasizes values such as honesty, respect, and empathy — principles that help students build a strong moral foundation for life.
